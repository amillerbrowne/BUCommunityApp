<p>The largest college at BU—with 24 departments—CAS is where you’ll find undergraduate programs in the humanities, natural and computational sciences, and social sciences.</p>

	<ul class="links"><li class="website"><a class="button" href="http://www.bu.edu/cas/">College of Arts &amp; Sciences</a></li><li class="bulletin"><a class="button" href="/academics/cas/">View Bulletin</a></li></ul>

<p>With nearly 40 areas of humanities, natural and computational sciences, and social sciences to choose from, GRS is home to approximately 1,800 graduate degree candidates and faculty from many schools and colleges at BU.</p>

	<ul class="links"><li class="website"><a class="button" href="http://www.bu.edu/cas/academics/graduate-education/">Graduate School of Arts &amp; Sciences</a></li><li class="bulletin"><a class="button" href="/academics/grs/">View Bulletin</a></li></ul>

</div>

<p>From Mass Communication, Advertising &amp; Public Relations, to Journalism, to Film &amp; Television, COM’s departments offer graduate and undergraduate programs dedicated to training well-rounded professional communicators.</p>

	<ul class="links"><li class="website"><a class="button" href="http://www.bu.edu/com/">College of Communication</a></li><li class="bulletin"><a class="button" href="/academics/com/">View Bulletin</a></li></ul>

</div>

<div class="school last"><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-8394" src="/academics/files/2011/08/ENG-small.jpg" alt="" width="206" height="127" /><h3>College of Engineering</h3>

<p>Choose from programs in Biomedical, Electrical &amp; Computer, Mechanical, and Systems Engineering and Materials Science &amp; Engineering. Whatever the topic, ENG is focused on advancing the frontiers of science and technology through research, discovery, and innovation.</p>

	<ul class="links"><li class="website"><a class="button" href="http://www.bu.edu/eng/">College of Engineering</a></li><li class="bulletin"><a class="button" href="/academics/eng/">View Bulletin</a></li></ul>

</div>

<div class="school"><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-8393" src="/academics/files/2011/08/CFA-small.jpg" alt="" width="206" height="127" /><h3>College of Fine Arts</h3>

<p>CFA’s three schools—Music, Theatre, and Visual Arts—form an intense, dynamic arts community combining professional, individualized training at the graduate and undergraduate levels with collaborative work that increasingly crosses departments and schools.</p>

	<ul class="links"><li class="website"><a class="button" href="http://www.bu.edu/cfa/">College of Fine Arts</a></li><li class="bulletin"><a class="button" href="/academics/cfa/">View Bulletin</a></li></ul>

</div>

<div class="school"><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-8393" src="/academics/files/2011/08/CGS-small.jpg" alt="" width="206" height="127" /><h3>College of General Studies</h3>

<p>This two-year college offers an integrated liberal arts core curriculum, taught through a collaborative team structure. From here, students continue into specific majors in the University’s various liberal arts and professional degree programs.</p>

	<ul class="links"><li class="website"><a class="button" href="http://www.bu.edu/cgs/">College of General Studies</a></li><li class="bulletin"><a class="button" href="/academics/cgs/">View Bulletin</a></li></ul>

</div>

<p>Four departments—Health Sciences, Occupational Therapy, Physical Therapy &amp; Athletic Training, and Speech, Language &amp; Hearing Sciences—offer programs focused on professional training and cutting-edge research. Students doing fieldwork benefit from a clinical network of more than 1,400 affiliations worldwide—400 of them in Greater Boston.</p>

	<ul class="links"><li class="website"><a class="button" href="http://www.bu.edu/sargent/">College of Health &amp; Rehabilitation Sciences: Sargent College</a></li><li class="bulletin"><a class="button" href="/academics/sar/">View Bulletin</a></li></ul>

</div>

<div class="school"><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-8393" src="/academics/files/2011/08/MET-small.jpg" alt="" width="206" height="127" /><h3>Metropolitan College</h3>

<p>Choose from more than 60 full- and part-time degree and certificate programs, as well as noncredit courses of study and an undergraduate degree completion program. On-campus, online, and blended programs in fields as varied as literature, computer science, management,criminal justice, and gastronomy—to name a few—are available to MET students.</p>

	<ul class="links"><li class="website"><a class="button" href="http://www.bu.edu/met/">Metropolitan College</a></li><li class="bulletin"><a class="button" href="/academics/met/">View Bulletin</a></li></ul>

</div>

<p>SDM offers the Doctor of Dental Medicine (a traditional four-year and advanced-standing two-year) and advanced certificates and degrees, such as CAGS, MSD, DSc, and DScD, in all recognized specialties. Qualified undergrads can enroll in the seven-year program to earn a BA or BS from CAS and a DMD.</p>

	<ul class="links"><li class="website"><a class="button" href="http://www.bu.edu/dental/">Henry M. Goldman School of Dental Medicine</a></li><li class="bulletin"><a class="button" href="/academics/sdm/">View Bulletin</a></li></ul>

</div>

<p>Grads and undergrads choose a concentration from more than 20 areas, including early childhood education, higher education, counseling, and policy. The school’s many collaborative arrangements provide myriad professional training and practica opportunities.</p>

	<ul class="links"><li class="website"><a class="button" href="http://www.bu.edu/sed/">School of Education</a></li><li class="bulletin"><a class="button" href="/academics/sed/">View Bulletin</a></li></ul>

<p>Dedicated to advancing human progress and improving the human condition, and with a deep commitment to interdisciplinarity, the Pardee School – housed within CAS and GRS – offers 5 undergraduate majors, 8 undergraduate minors, 9 graduate degrees, and 2 graduate certificates in international relations and regional studies.</p>

	<ul class="links"><li class="website"><a class="button" href="http://www.bu.edu/pardeeschool/">Frederick S. Pardee School of Global Studies</a></li><li class="bulletin"><a class="button" href="/academics/frederick-s-pardee-school-of-global-studies/">View Bulletin</a></li></ul>

</div>

	<ul class="links"><li class="website"><a class="button" href="http://www.bu.edu/sha/">School of Hospitality</a></li><li class="bulletin"><a class="button" href="/academics/sha/">View Bulletin</a></li></ul>

</div>

	<ul class="links"><li class="website"><a class="button" href="http://www.bu.edu/law/">School of Law</a></li><li class="bulletin"><a class="button" href="/academics/law/">View Bulletin</a></li></ul>
